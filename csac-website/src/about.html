About the Civil Services and Aspirants Club

Establishment

  • The club was established in 2014 by Rahul Javir Sir (IAS, Assam cadre), an esteemed alumnus of GCOEARA, whose vision was to guide students towards excellence, leadership, and civil service awareness. His initiative laid a strong foundation for nurturing talent and inspiring many aspirants across the campus.
  • In year 2021 our alumni Devashish Chhatre and Pratik Bhosale revived it again
  • Now N. P. Wagh mam is an incharge of our club CSAC GCOEARA .
  • We have support and guidance from more than 40 officers from various different posts

Vision

To build a team of top achievers in competitive exams through dedication and mutual support.

Mission

To make students capable of unlocking their full potential through guidance, self-improvement, and focused preparation for the exams like UPSC, MPSC, CDS, AFCAT,NDA.
